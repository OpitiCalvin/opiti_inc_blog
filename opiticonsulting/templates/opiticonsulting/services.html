Our Services

GIS and Remote Sensing

"A map is worth more than a thousand words."

We help collect, organise and manage your spatial/geographic data, attibute information and related information in the form of easy to understand and interpret maps. From field collected data to information extraction from satellite imagery, our services are tailored towards efficient and effective data management and extraction, with detailed metadata capture, enabling use in raw and post-processed forms.

Spatial Analytics

"Everything is related to everything else, but near things are more related" - Waldo R. Tobler

Make sense of you data - business, social, etc - through its analysis and determination of its characteristincs and relationships with reference to space or geography.

At Opiti Consulting, we apply the requisite exploratory spatial data and spatial analysis tools and algorithms, such as spatial dependence, spatial autocorrelaton, Inverse Distance Weighting, Kriging, Geographic Weighted Regression (to name a few), through available tools and software - ESRI's ArcGIS, QGIS, GeoDa, Python and R - to provide an indepth insight to data and its relationship and distribution in space.

Data Science and Analysis

"No data is useless. It might seem so today, but in the long run or the future, it will be your most valuable asset."

Our data science and analytics services involves the analysis of your historical and geo-enriched datasets to make predictions about the future. The ability to determine business, consumer, market etc trends is critical in re-aligning an enterprise towards success.

Big data, IoT, data mining and machine/data learning are the technologies of the inteconnected future. We offer these services as we also provide technical know-how, infrastructure and tools to facilitate your productivity and efficiency in you operations.

Programming and Solution Development

Our solutions and services are geared towards enterprises and resource sharing. We do these and more through development of web-based and mobile solutions that are accessible both off-line and online.
